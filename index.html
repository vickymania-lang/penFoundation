Our Vision

Our vision is that the pupils will be given every opportunity to demonstrate creativity, challenge their own capabilities, appreciate individual differences and become life long learners

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an enabling environment that will facilitate the provisions of a sound development of every child with excellence through diligence, honesty, discipline, intergrity and respect for others and constituted authority.


Welcome to Pen Foundation School

The school named pen foundation nursery and primary is a dream and vision of that was revealed by Jesus Christ himself to Mrs Nwokoye-ejike Anne. his branch at Iba later gave birth to another branch at Army post service estate. The entire family agreed and it later turned to a collective wish of the family to affect humanity in a positive manner. The birth of the school in September 18th 2006 was thus a fulfilment of a dream.This branch at Iba later gave birth to another brand at Army post service estate. In September 2020,the school introduced secondary classes Jss one-Jss three. Located in a modest and serene environment of Iba housing Estate, Ojo Lagos, the founder Mrs Nwokoye-ejike Anne believes that pen foundation school, daycare, nursery, primary and college should be a reputable standard.
